Orinari ("we", "our", or "us") is a meal planning and pantry management application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information, such as your email address
  • Profile and preference data, such as dietary preferences and cuisine choices
  • Pantry items, grocery lists, recipes, and meal plans

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • App usage data, such as feature usage and interactions
  • Device and diagnostic information
  • Error logs and performance data

1.3 AI and Image Processing Data

  • Images you upload for scanning, such as receipts or pantry items
  • Text extracted from images (OCR)
  • AI-generated outputs, such as cleaned or structured data

1.4 Subscription and Payment Information

  • Subscription status and entitlement data
  • Purchase tokens and billing metadata

Payments are processed securely via Google Play Billing. We do not store your credit card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core app functionality such as meal planning and pantry tracking
  • To sync your data across sessions
  • To enable AI-powered features you choose to use
  • To manage subscriptions and entitlements
  • To improve performance, reliability, and user experience

3. AI Features and Processing

Orinari includes optional AI-powered features such as receipt scanning and data extraction. These features process user-provided content to generate results.

  • AI features are only activated when you explicitly use them
  • Usage may be limited based on your subscription plan
  • AI processing may involve third-party infrastructure

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share data with trusted service providers solely to operate the app, including:

  • Cloud infrastructure providers for app hosting and backend services
  • Payment processors such as Google Play
  • AI and processing services required for app features

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the service.

  • You may delete your account at any time from within the app
  • Account deletion removes associated personal data

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure.

7. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Stop using AI features at any time

8. Children’s Privacy

Orinari is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

10. Account Deletion

To delete your account:

  1. Open the Orinari app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
