Orinari

Smart Kitchen & Meals

Orinari is a smart kitchen and meal planning app that helps users manage pantry items, organize groceries, save recipes, and plan meals in one place.

Users can sign in with Google to securely access their account, sync their personal data, and use app features across sessions and devices.

What Orinari does

Orinari helps users manage everyday kitchen tasks with tools for pantry tracking, grocery organization, recipe storage, and meal planning.

Google Sign-In

Google Sign-In is used to create and access user accounts securely so users can return to their saved pantry items, recipes, grocery lists, subscriptions, and meal plans.

Optional AI Features

Orinari may provide optional AI-powered tools such as receipt or pantry scanning to help users structure and organize kitchen information more efficiently.
