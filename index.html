Privacy Policy for Orinari

Last updated: April 2, 2026

How We Use Information

  • To sync your data and manage your account

Sharing

We may use service providers to operate app functionality, payments, analytics, and infrastructure. We do not sell your personal information.

Data Retention and Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data using the in-app delete account feature, where available.

Security

Children

The app is not directed to children unless explicitly stated otherwise.
