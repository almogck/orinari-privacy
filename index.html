Orinari

Orinari is a smart kitchen and meal planning app that helps users manage pantry items, organize groceries, plan meals, and keep recipes in one place.

Users can sign in with Google to create an account and securely access their personal pantry, recipes, grocery lists, meal plans, and subscription features across sessions.

Orinari may also offer optional AI-powered features such as scanning receipts or pantry items to help users organize kitchen information more efficiently.
