Privacy Policy for Orinari

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Orinari is a meal planning and pantry management app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, what choices you have, and how you can request deletion of your account and associated data.

Information We Collect

  • Account and profile information, including your email address and account details
  • Pantry, grocery, recipe, and meal-planning data you create
  • Subscription and entitlement information
  • Diagnostic and audit information related to AI features and app operations

How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve the app
  • To sync your data and manage your account
  • To support subscriptions and premium features
  • To operate AI-powered features you choose to use
  • To maintain app security, reliability, and abuse prevention

Sharing

We may use service providers to operate app functionality, payments, analytics, hosting, and infrastructure. We do not sell your personal information.

Data Retention and Deletion

You can request deletion of your Orinari account and associated data using the in-app Delete Account feature in Settings or by contacting us using the email below.

Account Deletion for Orinari

If you created an Orinari account, you can request deletion of your account and associated data in one of the following ways:

Option 1 — Delete your account in the app

  1. Open the Orinari app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion request

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

Email almog.ck@gmail.com with the subject line Orinari Account Deletion Request and include the email address associated with your account.

What data is deleted

When your deletion request is processed, we delete the data associated with your account, including:

  • Account/profile data
  • Pantry items
  • Grocery items
  • Recipes you created in the app
  • Preference data
  • Subscription entitlement records
  • Cooking history/events
  • AI action/audit log records associated with your account

What may be retained

We may retain limited information if required for legitimate purposes such as security, fraud prevention, chargeback handling, legal compliance, or enforcing our terms. If any such retention applies, it will be limited to what is necessary.

Partial deletion requests

At this time, Orinari does not offer a separate self-service workflow for deleting specific categories of data without deleting the full account.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit where supported by our service providers and infrastructure, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children

Orinari is not directed to children.

Contact

For privacy questions or account deletion requests, contact: almog.ck@gmail.com
