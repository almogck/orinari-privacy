<h1>Privacy Policy for Orinari</h1>
<p class="muted">Last updated: April 2026</p>

<p>
Orinari ("we", "our", or "us") is a meal planning and pantry management application.
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.
</p>

<h2>1. Information We Collect</h2>

<h3>1.1 Information You Provide</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Account information (such as email address)</li>
  <li>Profile and preference data (dietary preferences, cuisine choices)</li>
  <li>Pantry items, grocery lists, recipes, and meal plans</li>
</ul>

<h3>1.2 Automatically Collected Information</h3>
<ul>
  <li>App usage data (feature usage, interactions)</li>
  <li>Device and diagnostic information</li>
  <li>Error logs and performance data</li>
</ul>

<h3>1.3 AI and Image Processing Data</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Images you upload for scanning (e.g. receipts or pantry items)</li>
  <li>Text extracted from images (OCR)</li>
  <li>AI-generated outputs (such as cleaned or structured data)</li>
</ul>

<h3>1.4 Subscription and Payment Information</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Subscription status and entitlement data</li>
  <li>Purchase tokens and billing metadata</li>
</ul>
<p>
Payments are processed securely via Google Play Billing. We do not store your credit card details.
</p>

---

<h2>2. How We Use Your Information</h2>
<ul>
  <li>To provide core app functionality (meal planning, pantry tracking)</li>
  <li>To sync your data across sessions</li>
  <li>To enable AI-powered features you choose to use</li>
  <li>To manage subscriptions and entitlements</li>
  <li>To improve performance, reliability, and user experience</li>
</ul>

---

<h2>3. AI Features and Processing</h2>
<p>
Orinari includes optional AI-powered features such as receipt scanning and data extraction.
These features process user-provided content to generate results.
</p>

<ul>
  <li>AI features are only activated when you explicitly use them</li>
  <li>Usage may be limited based on your subscription plan</li>
  <li>AI processing may involve third-party infrastructure</li>
</ul>

---

<h2>4. Data Sharing</h2>
<p>We do not sell your personal data.</p>

<p>We may share data with trusted service providers solely to operate the app, including:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Cloud infrastructure providers (app hosting and backend services)</li>
  <li>Payment processors (Google Play)</li>
  <li>AI and processing services required for app features</li>
</ul>

---

<h2>5. Data Retention</h2>
<p>
We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the service.
</p>

<ul>
  <li>You may delete your account at any time from within the app</li>
  <li>Account deletion removes associated personal data</li>
</ul>

<h2>6. Data Security</h2>
<p>
We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data.
However, no system is completely secure.
</p>

<h2>7. Your Rights</h2>
